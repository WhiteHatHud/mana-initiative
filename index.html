Who we are

Tullab al-'Ilm — seekers of knowledge.

We are a group of Muslim students from Institutes of Higher Learning in Singapore, aspiring to be tullab al-'ilm — seeking truth, living by it, and protecting ourselves from falsehood. We meet roughly once a month to read and discuss texts on Islam, theology, Sufism, and philosophy, guided by qualified Asatizah.

Everyone with the intention to genuinely learn is welcome — whether you've studied Islam formally or not.

Our objectives

Why Ma'na exists.

01

Revive the knowledge culture

To renew and revive the culture of seeking beneficial Islamic knowledge among Muslim students in Institutes of Higher Learning.

02

Islam in the context of our times

To increase our understanding of Islam in the context of our times — navigating modernity, university life, and the questions our generation faces.

Why join

You belong here.

Whether you're deeply familiar with Islamic texts or just beginning to explore — there is a seat for you at Ma'na.

A safe & comfortable space

A judgement-free environment for all. Ask hard questions. Share your doubts. No one is expected to have all the answers.

Right & proper sources

We read from carefully selected, authoritative Islamic texts — classical and contemporary — guided by qualified Asatizah.

Deeper understanding

Move beyond surface-level knowledge. Appreciate the richness of Islamic thought — theology, Sufism, philosophy, and ethics.

Navigate doubts with clarity

Increase awareness and confidence in your faith. Find grounded, scholarly perspectives on the questions university raises.
