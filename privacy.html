Privacy Policy

How The Ma'na Initiative collects and uses your information.

Last updated: June 2026. This policy describes how The Ma'na Initiative ("we", "us", "our") handles personal information collected on this website.

What we collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Registration: name, email, school/IHL, preferred medium, optional notes — to manage session attendance.
  • Subscribe: email address (and optionally phone) — to send session announcements.
  • Join interest: name, email, school/IHL, optional motivation — to welcome you to the community.
  • Contact form: name, email, message — to respond to your enquiry.

We do not collect payment information, run advertising, or sell data to third parties.

How we use it

  • To send session announcements and reminders (if you subscribed).
  • To manage registrations for sessions.
  • To respond to contact form enquiries.
  • For internal planning (session attendance, community size).

Cookies

We use a single cookie/localStorage entry (mana_cookie_consent) to remember your cookie preference. No tracking cookies, no advertising pixels.

Data storage

Data is stored in Supabase (hosted Postgres) in Singapore or the nearest available region. Access is restricted to committee members via authenticated login. Data is not shared with third parties except as required by law.

Committee member photos & names

We publish a committee member's name and photo only after obtaining their explicit consent. Members may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing hello@mana-initiative.sg.

Contact

Questions about this policy: hello@mana-initiative.sg
